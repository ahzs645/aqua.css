Overall Recommendation

Consider merging the best of both:

  • Use Showcase's variable system, mixins, checkbox (with indeterminate), progress (with variants), and context menu
  • Use src's menu bar (with dropdowns), alert dialog (with modal), and tooltip (data-attribute API)
  • Keep both radio and toolbar styles as they serve different aesthetics
